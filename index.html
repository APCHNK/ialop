About IALOP

International Association of Licensed Online Pharmacies, Retail Pharmacies

Since its inception in 2015, IALOP has maintained a 100% perfect patient safety record.

We team up with rule-makers, drugstore workers, doctors, patients, government folks, and customer groups. We do this to constantly improve how mail-order pharmacy websites work. This helps keep patients safe and save money.

Safe & Affordable Prescription Drugs

Each IALOP pharmacy member is licensed and regulated by the government for safety.

We sell medicines at prices that are up to 70% lower than in U.S. drugstores. Just like U.S. mail-order businesses, IALOP pharmacies need a real prescription from your doctor. We give the same quality and easy service as U.S. mail-order businesses. This means we can send name-brand medicines straight to your home. This is good because medicine prices in the U.S. are very high.

You ask and we answer

Frequently Asked Questions?

What brand name manufacturers do you have?

Safe online drugstores approved by IALOP have prescription medicines from big worldwide companies like Pfizer, Merck, Eli Lilly, and many more.

What range of savings can I expect?

IALOP sells medicines made by top brand-name companies. These are up to 70% cheaper than what they cost in the U.S.

Are all medications shipped from Canada?

IALOP members sell medicines approved by Health Canada from their legal pharmacies in Canada. They also work with approved pharmacies and checked packing places in other parts of the world. These places sell medicines and send them straight to patients from where they are. Patients choose where their medicines come from, and this is checked with them when they buy.

How can I make sure a website is IALOP certified?

Websites approved by IALOP show the IALOP badge as seen below. Because people trust this badge, some websites have copied it and put it on their own pages. These websites pretend to be part of IALOP, but they are not. Always double-check on the IALOP.com website to make sure a medicine-selling site is really a part of IALOP.
