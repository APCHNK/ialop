About IALOP

International Association of
Licensed Online Pharmacies

IALOP's 18 Year Commitment to Safe Online Pharmacy Certification

Started in 2015, IALOP has spent 18 years teaming up with rule-makers, drugstore workers, doctors, patients, government folks, and customer groups. Together, they work to make mail-order pharmacy websites better and safer for patients, and also to help patients save money.

We give you information about safe online drugstore websites that are legal, approved, and trustworthy.

IALOP thinks that every person in the world should be able to buy medicines they can afford to keep them healthy, happy, and well. This is what IALOP works to protect every day.

IALOP is an International Association of Licensed Online Pharmacies, Retail Pharmacies.

We sell medicines and ongoing treatment drugs (but not drugs that are tightly controlled) in amounts that last 30 to 90 days to people in Canada and the U.S. Every IALOP pharmacy member is approved and checked by the government to make sure they're safe.

IALOP Sells Medicines Made by Big Brand Companies. These Medicines Are up to 70% Cheaper Than in the U.S.

We include medicines from big worldwide companies like Pfizer, Merck, Eli Lilly, and many more. These medicines have been checked and shown to be safe. Actually, since we started in 2015, IALOP has given medicines to over ten million U.S. patients, and we've never had a safety problem.

IALOP Drugstores Make Sure Patients Can Get Good-Quality Prescription Medicines Without Spending Too Much Money.

Our prices are up to 70% cheaper than what you'd pay for similar medicines at U.S. drugstores. Just like U.S. mail-order businesses, IALOP pharmacies need a real prescription from your doctor. We give the same quality and easy service as U.S. mail-order businesses. This means we can send name-brand medicines straight to your home. This makes IALOP a quick and believable solution to the very high medicine prices in the U.S.
