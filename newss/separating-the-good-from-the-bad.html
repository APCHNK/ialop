Separating the Good from the Bad

September 29, 2015

The International Association of Licensed Online Pharmacies (IALOP) often gets calls and emails from people who get unwanted email offers with super low prices on various products, especially drugs for improving male performance. We're glad to say that IALOP members never send spam emails and never share the private details of our patients with anyone.

You can trust IALOP pharmacies for their safe practices, but there are dishonest businesses online that try to trick people into unsafe deals. Spam emails from these bad people went down a lot a year ago when the Spamit program was shut down, but it's still a problem and is getting worse.

IALOP thinks it's important to share information about these spam senders and the group of dishonest pharmacies behind them. This article from Brian Krebs at Krebs on Security is a great starting point to learn about the sneaky methods of people who take advantage of unsuspecting patients. We're lucky that IALOP is here to keep consumers safe.
